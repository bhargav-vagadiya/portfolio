Project: ATLAS: the Carbon App
Bhargav Vagadiya developed ATLAS, the Carbon App, a Flutter application for carbon tracking and environmental analytics. Bhargav served as Lead Developer on the ATLAS Carbon App at Tagline Infotech LLP. Bhargav integrated the AppsFlyer SDK in ATLAS for user analytics, event tracking, and attribution monitoring. He implemented multi-platform Firebase authentication in ATLAS supporting Google login, Apple login, and Email login. Bhargav developed a responsive UI in ATLAS using flutter_screenutil for a consistent cross-device experience across different screen sizes. He integrated Google Play in-app subscriptions in ATLAS with receipt validation for Android users. He integrated Apple App Store in-app subscriptions in ATLAS with receipt validation for iOS users.
