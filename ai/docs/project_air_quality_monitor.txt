Project: Air Quality Monitor app
Bhargav Vagadiya contributed as a team member to an Air Quality Monitor application built with Flutter. The Air Quality Monitor app connects to ESP32 BLE devices using Flutter method channels for native Android and iOS Bluetooth Low Energy connectivity. Bhargav implemented TCP server communication in the Air Quality Monitor app for real-time air quality data retrieval from Cubic AM 7000 hardware devices. He built data visualizations in the Air Quality Monitor app for CO2 levels, PM2.5 particulate matter, and temperature metrics collected from the Cubic AM 7000 sensor.
